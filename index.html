About Takumi

Takumi is a young man going up in the late 90's in Japan. He works for his father's tofu shop as a delivery driver. His delivery route is one of japan's many tenchnical mountainous driving roads. His dilivery vehicle, a seemingly underwelming Toyota Corolla. An early exposer to driving along with many nudges from his father has pushed his skills as a driver and primed him in becoming a down hill racing proteje.
